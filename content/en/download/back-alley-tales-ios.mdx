<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Configuration Guide">

To successfully configure the game client on your device, follow the sequence of steps detailed below. Skipping any of these steps may result in an "Untrusted Enterprise Developer" error when attempting to launch the application.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Download the Profile">
    Open the Safari browser on your iOS device and navigate to the verified download portal. Tap the download link to fetch the configuration profile. A system prompt will ask for permission to download the profile. Tap **Allow** to proceed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Locate System Settings">
    Once the download completes, exit your browser and open the native iOS **Settings** app. You should see a new notification banner at the top of the settings menu labeled **Profile Downloaded**. Tap this banner directly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Install the Profile">
    In the profile installation screen, tap **Install** in the top-right corner. Enter your device passcode when prompted. Read the consent screen, then tap **Install** again at the bottom of the screen to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Trust the Developer Certificate">
    If the profile requires enterprise developer trust, navigate to **Settings** > **General** > **VPN & Device Management**. Under the Enterprise App section, select the developer name associated with the profile and tap **Trust**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Verification Successful">
Once trusted, the developer label will change status to "Trusted". You can now return to your home screen and launch the game icon.
</Callout>

During the installation process, you may encounter system alerts or installation blocks. Refer to the troubleshooting matrix below to resolve these issues quickly.

| Issue | Possible Cause | Recommended Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Profile Not Downloading** | Safari block or poor connection | Clear Safari cache and retry over Wi-Fi |
| **Missing Profile Banner** | Download failed to register | Go to General > VPN & Device Management manually |
| **Verification Failed Error** | Certificate revoked by Apple | Delete the profile and download an updated version |
| **App Instantly Crashes** | Insufficient system RAM | Restart device and close all background apps |
